Thank you for using ICS Importer. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how your information is handled.

In short:
ICS Importer helps you import calendar events from .ics files into your Google Calendar. The app does not store your calendar data or personal information on our servers. All data flows directly between your device and Google's services.

1. What This App Does

ICS Importer is a productivity tool that allows you to:

  • Import calendar events from .ics (iCalendar) files into your Google Calendar
  • Preview events before importing them
  • Automatically import events to a default calendar of your choice

The app is designed to be simple, secure, and respectful of your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we access:

  • Email address and profile name: Used for authentication purposes only
  • Google Calendar access: Required to retrieve your calendar list and create events

Important: This information is not stored by our app. It is handled securely by Google's authentication system.

2.2 Calendar Data

The app accesses:

  • List of your Google Calendars (names and IDs)
  • Calendar events being imported (titles, dates, times, descriptions, locations, attendees)

Storage: Calendar data is not permanently stored by the app. Events are processed in memory and sent directly to Google Calendar. Your calendar list is fetched dynamically when needed.

2.3 User Preferences

The app stores the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Auto-import setting (enabled/disabled)
  • Default calendar selection (calendar ID and name)

These preferences are stored in your device's local storage (Android SharedPreferences) and are not transmitted to any external server.

2.4 File Access

When you select or open an .ics file:

  • The app reads the file contents to extract calendar event data
  • Files are processed temporarily in memory only
  • No permanent copy of the file is stored

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Authentication: To verify your Google account
  • Calendar Import: To create events in your selected Google Calendar
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences (auto-import and default calendar)

We do not use your data for any other purpose, including analytics, advertising, or marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Sign-In API

Provider: Google LLC
Purpose: User authentication
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Google Calendar API

Provider: Google LLC
Purpose: Retrieve calendar list and create calendar events
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions: The app requests access to your Google Calendar (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar). You can revoke this access at any time in your Google Account settings.

4.3 Advertising (Planned Feature)

Note: Advertising functionality using Google AdMob will be integrated in a future version of the app.

When AdMob is implemented, it may collect:

  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • App usage data (app opens, ad interactions)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)

You will be able to reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings. For EU/EEA users, a GDPR consent mechanism will be provided.

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Ad Settings: https://myadcenter.google.com

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 No Remote Storage

Important: ICS Importer does not operate its own backend servers. All calendar data flows directly from your device to Google's servers via secure HTTPS connections.

We never receive, store, or process your calendar data on any servers owned or operated by us.

5.2 Local Storage

Only the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • User preferences (auto-import setting, default calendar)

This data is stored unencrypted in Android SharedPreferences, as it contains no sensitive personal information.

5.3 Security Measures

  • Encrypted Communications: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0: Industry-standard authentication protocol; the app does not handle or store passwords
  • App Sandbox: Android's app sandbox isolates the app's data from other apps
  • Minimal Permissions: The app requests only the minimum necessary permissions

6. Permissions Required

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to communicate with Google authentication and Calendar APIs, and (in future) to load advertisements
  • Post Notifications (Android 13+): Used to notify you when auto-import completes. You can deny this permission; auto-import will still work, but no notification will be shown

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Sign In/Out: You can sign in or out of your Google account at any time within the app
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the app's access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Delete App Data: You can clear all app data (including preferences) via Android Settings > Apps > ICS Importer > Storage > Clear Data
  • Delete Imported Events: You can delete any events imported by the app directly in Google Calendar
  • Disable Notifications: You can disable or control app notifications in your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

ICS Importer is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

9. International Users and Compliance

9.1 GDPR (European Union)

For users in the EU/EEA, we process your data based on:

  • User Consent: For accessing your Google Calendar
  • Legitimate Interest: For providing app functionality

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data, as well as to object to processing. When AdMob is implemented, explicit consent will be required for personalized ads.

9.2 CCPA (California, USA)

For California residents:

  • Personal information collected includes email and calendar data
  • This information is used for service functionality only
  • We do not sell your personal information
  • You have the right to access and delete your personal information

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Notable future changes include:

  • Integration of Google AdMob advertising
  • Potential addition of analytics or crash reporting

The updated policy will always be available at this same link. We recommend checking it periodically. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates when the policy was last updated.
